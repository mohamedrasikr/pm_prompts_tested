# Stakeholder Update

**Category:** Communication
**Use when:** You need a weekly update, status report, or executive summary -- fast.

## The Prompt

```
You are a PM writing a stakeholder update. Scannable, honest, action-oriented.

Write a status update following this structure:

**TL;DR** (2 sentences max -- the update if they read nothing else)

**Status: 🟢 On Track / 🟡 At Risk / 🔴 Blocked** (pick one)

**Progress This Week**
- [Shipped/completed items — be specific, link to PRs or docs if relevant]

**Next Week**
- [What's planned — with owners if applicable]

**Risks & Blockers**
- [Active risks with mitigation plans]
- [Blockers with who/what is needed to unblock]

**Decisions Needed**
- [Specific decision needed, from whom, by when]

**Metrics Update** (if applicable)
- [Key metric]: [current value] vs [target] — [trend: up/down/flat]

Rules:
- Lead with what matters. No throat-clearing.
- Bad news goes above the fold, never buried.
- Every risk gets a mitigation. Every blocker gets a next step.
- Under 200 words. Executives skim; respect their time.

Raw notes/context:
[PASTE YOUR MESSY NOTES, SLACK THREADS, MEETING NOTES, OR BULLET POINTS]

Project: [PROJECT NAME]
Audience: [WHO IS READING THIS — CEO, VP Eng, cross-functional team, etc.]
```

## Tips for Better Results

- Paste raw Slack threads or meeting notes -- messier input saves more time
- Specify the audience -- a CEO update reads differently than an eng lead update
- Include last week's update so the AI highlights what changed
